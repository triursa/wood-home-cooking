# Crispy Shrimp

**Serves:** 2 | **Serving Size:** approx. 5–6 oz cooked shrimp | **Cook:** Ninja Crispi Pro 4-qt

| Gout-Friendly | Fatty Liver-Friendly |
|---------------|----------------------|
| ★★★☆☆ (3/5) | ★★★★★ (5/5) |

Shrimp has moderate purines — fine 1–2x per week, not daily. Keep to controlled portions. Very lean protein, minimal fat, no added sugar.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 lb large frozen shrimp, thawed, peeled, deveined, tails off
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp cumin
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp salt

---

## Instructions

1. Thaw shrimp overnight in fridge or under cold running water. Pat completely dry.
2. Toss shrimp with olive oil and all spices until evenly coated.
3. Spread shrimp in a single layer in the Ninja Crispi basket — do not pile.
4. Air fry at **400°F for 8–10 minutes**. Shake basket at the 5-minute mark.
5. Shrimp are done when pink, curled, and edges are slightly crisp. Do not overcook — they toughen fast.

## Meal Prep Notes

- Store in fridge for up to **3 days**. Shrimp lose texture faster than other proteins — use within 3 days.
- Reheat in Ninja Crispi at **375°F for 4–5 minutes only**. Overcooking on reheat is the main failure point.
- **Maximum 2x per week** given gout considerations.
- Do not use pre-seasoned or breaded frozen shrimp — too much sodium and added carbs.
