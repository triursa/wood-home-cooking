<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul class="ingredients-list">
  <li>1 lb large frozen shrimp, thawed, peeled, deveined, tails off</li>
  <li>1 tbsp olive oil</li>
  <li>½ tsp garlic powder</li>
  <li>½ tsp paprika</li>
  <li>¼ tsp cumin</li>
  <li>¼ tsp black pepper</li>
  <li>¼ tsp salt</li>
</ul>

<h2>Instructions</h2>
<ol class="steps-list">
  <li>Thaw shrimp overnight in fridge or under cold running water. Pat completely dry.</li>
  <li>Toss shrimp with olive oil and all spices until evenly coated.</li>
  <li>Spread in a single layer in the Ninja Crispi basket — do not pile.</li>
  <li>Air fry at 400°F for 8–10 minutes. Shake basket at the 5-minute mark.</li>
  <li>Done when pink, curled, and edges are slightly crisp. Do not overcook — they toughen fast.</li>
</ol>

<div class="notes-box">
  <div class="notes-title">Meal Prep Notes</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Store in fridge for up to <strong>3 days</strong>. Shrimp lose texture faster than other proteins.</li>
    <li>Reheat at <strong>375°F for 4–5 minutes only</strong>. Overcooking on reheat is the main failure point.</li>
    <li><strong>Maximum 2x per week</strong> given gout considerations.</li>
    <li>Do not use pre-seasoned or breaded frozen shrimp.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
