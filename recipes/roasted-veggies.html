Roasted Veggies

Serves: 4 Serving: ~1 cup roasted Cook: Ninja Crispi Pro 4-qt
Gout-Friendly ★★★★★ Zero purines. High water content supports uric acid flushing.
Fatty Liver-Friendly ★★★★★ High fiber, no saturated fat, low sugar.

Nutrition per serving — ~1 cup

~120Calories
~4gProtein
~13gCarbs
~7gFat
~4gFiber
~160mgSodium
~5gSugar

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchini, cut into half-moons
  • 1 large head broccoli, cut into florets
  • 2 bell peppers, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut all vegetables into similar-sized pieces for even cooking.
  2. Toss vegetables with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  3. Add veggies to Ninja Crispi basket — they can all go in together, they shrink down.
  4. Air fry at 375°F for 18–20 minutes, shaking basket halfway through.
  5. Tender with slightly charred edges. Add 3–4 minutes if you prefer more char.
Meal Prep Notes
  • Store in airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days.
  • Reheat in Ninja Crispi at 375°F for 5–6 minutes.
  • Pairs with everything — chicken, shrimp, eggs, stir-fry.
  • Swap in yellow squash, asparagus, or cauliflower as needed.
