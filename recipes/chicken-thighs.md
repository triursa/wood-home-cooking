# Ninja Crispi Crispy Chicken Thighs

**Serves:** 4 | **Serving Size:** 2 thighs (approx. 6–7 oz cooked) | **Cook:** Ninja Crispi Pro 4-qt

| Gout-Friendly | Fatty Liver-Friendly |
|---------------|----------------------|
| ★★★★☆ (4/5) | ★★★★☆ (4/5) |

Low purine, no gout triggers. Boneless skinless keeps saturated fat minimal. Olive oil is a healthy fat.

---

## Ingredients

- 8 boneless skinless chicken thighs (approx. 3–3.5 lbs)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp kosher salt

---

## Instructions

1. Pat chicken thighs completely dry with paper towels — this is the key to crispy texture.
2. In a large bowl, toss thighs with olive oil, then coat evenly with all spices.
3. Preheat Ninja Crispi to 380°F for 3 minutes.
4. Place 4 thighs in a single layer in the basket (do not stack). Air fry at 380°F for 18–22 minutes, flipping halfway through.
5. Repeat for second batch.
6. Internal temp should reach 165°F. Rest 3 minutes before storing.

## Meal Prep Notes

- Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to **4 days**.
- Reheat in Ninja Crispi at **375°F for 5–8 minutes**, shaking halfway.
- Do not stack when reheating — lay flat for best texture.
- Cook in 2 batches of 4 — do not overcrowd the basket.
