<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul class="ingredients-list">
  <li>8 boneless skinless chicken thighs (approx. 3–3.5 lbs)</li>
  <li>2 tbsp olive oil</li>
  <li>1 tsp garlic powder</li>
  <li>1 tsp paprika</li>
  <li>½ tsp cumin</li>
  <li>½ tsp black pepper</li>
  <li>1 tsp kosher salt</li>
</ul>

<h2>Instructions</h2>
<ol class="steps-list">
  <li>Pat chicken thighs completely dry with paper towels — this is the key to crispy texture.</li>
  <li>In a large bowl, toss thighs with olive oil, then coat evenly with all spices.</li>
  <li>Preheat Ninja Crispi to 380°F for 3 minutes.</li>
  <li>Place 4 thighs in a single layer in the basket. Do not stack. Air fry at 380°F for 18–22 minutes, flipping halfway through.</li>
  <li>Repeat for the second batch of 4.</li>
  <li>Internal temp should reach 165°F. Rest 3 minutes before storing.</li>
</ol>

<div class="notes-box">
  <div class="notes-title">Meal Prep Notes</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Store in airtight container in the fridge for up to <strong>4 days</strong>.</li>
    <li>Reheat in Ninja Crispi at <strong>375°F for 5–8 minutes</strong>, shaking halfway.</li>
    <li>Do not stack when reheating — lay flat for best texture.</li>
    <li>Cook in 2 batches of 4 — do not overcrowd the basket.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
