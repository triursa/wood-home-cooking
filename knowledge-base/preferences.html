Preferences & Guidelines

The rules and conventions that apply to every recipe and meal plan in this system.

Dietary rules

RuleDetail

Batch cook structure

One batch cook per week: Monday

  • Monday batch covers all 5 weekday meals
  • Shrimp cooked fresh on cook nights — do not batch (texture degrades)
  • Thaw weekly protein portions in the fridge Friday night

Rating system

Gout-Friendly (1–5 ★)

Based on purine content and known gout dietary triggers.

RatingMeaning
★★★★★Very low purine, no known triggers
★★★★☆Low purine, minor considerations
★★★☆☆Moderate purine — limit frequency
★★☆☆☆High purine — use sparingly
★☆☆☆☆Avoid — significant gout risk

Known triggers to avoid: organ meats, most shellfish (shrimp in moderation is fine), alcohol, high-fructose corn syrup, excessive red meat.

Fatty Liver-Friendly (1–5 ★)

Based on saturated fat, added sugars, refined carbohydrates, and alcohol content.

RatingMeaning
★★★★★Ideal — minimal sat fat, sugar, refined carbs
★★★★☆Good — minor areas to watch
★★★☆☆Moderate — use with portion awareness
★★☆☆☆Use sparingly
★☆☆☆☆Avoid
