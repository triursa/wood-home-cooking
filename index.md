# Wood Home Cooking 🍳

Kaleb & Minny's meal prep system. Built around the **Ninja Crispi Pro**, two batch cooks a week, and eating in a way that supports managing Gout and Fatty Liver — without making every meal feel medicinal.

## How This Works

Every recipe includes:
- Estimated **calories and full nutrition facts** per serving
- A **Gout-Friendly rating** (1–5 ⭐) based on purine content and known triggers
- A **Fatty Liver-Friendly rating** (1–5 ⭐) based on saturated fat, added sugars, and refined carbs

Weekly meal plans target **~2,000 cal/day** with a **Sunday + Wednesday batch cook** structure.
